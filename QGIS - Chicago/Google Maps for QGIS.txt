How to add google satellite map to QGIS?
1) Right click on XYZ Tiles
2) Select New Connection
3) Add appropriate name and max and min zoom level that you want
4) Add the below mentioned URL
5) Click on OK

Google Maps: https://mt1.google.com/vt/lyrs=m&x={x}&y={y}&z={z}
Google Satellite: https://mt1.google.com/vt/lyrs=s&x={x}&y={y}&z={z}
Google Terrain: https://mt1.google.com/vt/lyrs=p&x={x}&y={y}&z={z}
